## Introduction

**Tsubaki** (camellia) is an Astro blog theme inspired by the beauty of Japanese aesthetics.

A warm cream palette `#F3F0EA` serves as the base for the entire design, evoking the natural texture of paper.

## Design Features

- **Zen Kaku Gothic New** — A Japanese font supporting ultra-thin to medium weights
- **Warm Gold** `#C9BC9C` — Used for buttons and accent colors
- **Fixed Side Strips** — Displaying the latest article and date
- **Triple Text Navigation** — Slide-up animation on hover

## Layout

Articles use a single-column layout with a maximum width of `720px`. No sidebar.
Generous whitespace and line spacing ensure optimal readability.

> "Whitespace, like silence, gives meaning to words."

## Getting Started

This theme is built with Astro. Content is placed in `src/content/blog/` as Markdown or MDX files.

```md
---
title: "Article Title"
titleEn: "Article Title"
description: "A brief description of the article"
date: 2026-03-17
tags: ["Tag 1", "Tag 2"]
---

Write your article content here.
```
