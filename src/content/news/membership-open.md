Registration for the 2026 Konokoto membership season is now open. New members who join before the end of March receive access to the full spring programme calendar, including the April market, two members-only evenings, and early notification of the summer residency.

The Konokoto programme has been running since 2022 as a way to support the long-term life of the district. Membership revenue directly funds the maintenance of shared spaces, the seasonal market infrastructure, and a small grants pool for neighborhood creative projects.

## 2026 membership tiers

**Community member** — Open registration, seasonal newsletter, market early access
**Supporting member** — All community benefits plus two invitations per quarter and a printed annual report
**Patron** — All supporting benefits plus name acknowledgment in programme materials and direct introductions to resident producers

Fees are listed on the Project page. Sliding-scale arrangements are available — contact us through the inquiry form to discuss.

## What the programme has funded

Over the past three years, Konokoto membership contributions have supported:

- Resurfacing of the north end of Tsubaki Square
- A small residency for two artists over two consecutive winters
- Equipment loans for four neighborhood events
- Printing costs for the community quarterly

Detailed accounts are available on request to all supporting members and patrons.

Visit the Project page to read about the full programme structure and register. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis; you will hear back within five working days.
