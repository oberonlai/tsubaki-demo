The Tsubaki Spring Market returns for its fourth year, bringing together local growers, artisans, and independent food producers for a single-day celebration of the season.

The market occupies the full length of Tsubaki Square and the adjoining lane, giving each vendor generous space to present their work properly.

**Date:** Sunday, April 5, 2026, 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
**Location:** Tsubaki Square, Main Street entrance
**Admission:** Free

## What to expect

This year's edition features over thirty stalls across four categories:

- **Fresh produce** — seasonal vegetables, herbs, and cut flowers from farms within fifty kilometers
- **Prepared foods** — baked goods, fermented pantry items, and light lunches
- **Craft and design** — ceramics, printed textiles, and handmade objects
- **Community** — neighborhood organizations, local publications, and project stalls

A small stage will host two acoustic performances during the afternoon session. Full programme to be announced by the end of March.

## Vendor applications

Applications for market stalls are open until March 28. Priority is given to first-time participants and producers from the immediate neighborhood.

To apply, use the inquiry form on the contact page and select "Other inquiries" — include "Spring Market Vendor" in your message.
